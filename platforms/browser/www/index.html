About Virtual Visual

To use this app you first must have an NFC facility in your phone and then have it turned on in your settings.

If you are ready you can scan a tag to find out where you are.

This app was made as a project for the BT Young Scientist competition 2015 by Timothy McGrath and Donal Brennan with the help of our mentor and deputy principal, Mr D. O' Reilly. The app is to help visually impaired people know where they are in a building by scanning their smartphone off an NFC tag (Near Field Communication tag) in the building.

Some of our classmates have helped with designing the logo for the app and we would like to thank them for their help on this.
